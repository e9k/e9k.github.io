 The Technocratic Colony

Tauo is a colony focused on science and technology. We seek military precision without engaging in warfare via smaller social structures and higher efficiency. The location of Tauo is currently being decided, but we will likely be based in Australia. We believe that rent is a form of slavery, since if you have to rent a house it means that you can't afford it and you are living above your means. Therefore, we are hoping to build a nomadic colony. We dont need to own land if we can mobilize our posessions like a military. Tauo can stay on any land, live in any environment.
